Egg parotha is a popular South Indian street food known for its rich flavors and satisfying textures. A flaky paratha is generously stuffed with a mixture of scrambled eggs, finely chopped onions, green chilies, and fresh cilantro. Pan-fried until crisp and golden brown, each bite offers a harmonious blend of savory and mildly spicy notes. Served hot, egg parotha is a comforting and indulgent treat enjoyed across the region for its delicious simplicity.
